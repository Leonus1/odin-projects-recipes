Couscous

An authentic recipe for Couscous with Seven Vegetables, the ultimate comfort food to enjoy with family and friends

ingredients:
  • 2.2 lb. couscous
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 large onion
  • 2.2 lb. lamb
Steps:
  1. Boil broth or water
  2. Toast the couscous in extra virgin olive oil
  3. Add couscous to boiling water
  4. Add some spices and herbs for flavor
