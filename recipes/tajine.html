Description

Chicken Tajine with vegetables

Ingredients:
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 8 chicken thighs, with bone and skin
  • 1 cup pitted green Moroccan or Greek olives
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, about 1/4 cup chopped
  • 1/2 bunch Italian parsley, about 1/4 cup chopped
Steps:
  1. On a work surface, halve the rutabaga. Cut each half into 8 wedges, then each wedge in two.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the rutabaga pieces in the oil. Add the eggplant, zucchini, bell pepper and spices. Stir well to coat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the tomato paste, harissa and broth. Pour over the vegetables. Add the remaining ingredients. Season lightly with salt as the preserved lemon and olives are quite salty. Season with pepper. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes over medium heat or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve with couscous or pita bread.
